# PΛNOPTICA
 
### Securing American Interests Abroad and at Home
 
**Watchtower** — an agentic AI platform that aggregates early signals and turns raw indicators into explainable, decision-ready intelligence.

## The Problem
 
Adversaries are swarming intelligence analysts with indicators and warnings faster than they can be handled. Analysts spend most of their time collecting and manually correlating data rather than acting on it — and the tempo gap is widening.
 
| Traditional analysis | Modern battlefield tempo |
| :---: | :---: |
| **8–10 hours** | **30 minutes** |
| legacy workflows | the real opportunity window |
 
The result is **decision paralysis**: pressure and an unclear picture force impulsive calls that put lives and assets at risk. Compounding this, off-the-shelf AI tends to throw Western solutions at non-Western problems, missing the cultural and structural nuance that makes intelligence actually usable.
 
> *"We are better today at collecting signals. I'm not sure we're any better at making sense of the signals we collect."*
> — Fortune, on the UnitedHealthcare CEO shooting
