## ▍THE PROBLEM

Adversaries are swarming analysts with indicators faster than they can be handled. Most analyst time goes to collecting and manually correlating data rather than acting on it. The tempo gap is widening.

<div align="center">

| Traditional analysis | Modern battlefield tempo |
| :---: | :---: |
| **8–10 hours** | **30 minutes** |
| legacy workflows | the real opportunity window |

</div>

The result is **decision paralysis**. Pressure and an unclear picture force impulsive calls that put lives and assets at risk. Compounding this, off-the-shelf AI throws Western solutions at non-Western problems, missing the cultural and structural nuance that makes intelligence usable.

> *"We are better today at collecting signals. I'm not sure we're any better at making sense of the signals we collect."*
> <br>— Fortune, on the UnitedHealthcare CEO shooting
