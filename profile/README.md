### THE PROBLEM

Adversaries are swarming analysts with indicators faster than they can be handled. Most analyst time goes to collecting and correlating rather than acting. The tempo gap is widening.

<div align="center">

| Traditional analysis | Modern battlefield tempo |
| :---: | :---: |
| **8–10 hrs** | **30 min** |
| legacy workflows | real opportunity window |

</div>

The result is **decision paralysis**. Pressure and an unclear picture force impulsive calls that put lives and assets at risk. Off-the-shelf AI compounds it, throwing Western solutions at non-Western problems and missing the structural nuance that makes intelligence usable.

> *"We are better today at collecting signals. I'm not sure we're any better at making sense of the signals we collect."*
